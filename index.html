Air Defense System

(School Safe!)

Project Summary

Does your classroom have issues with problematic students constantly throwing paper airplanes everywhere? Even at their peers? Sounds dangerous to us. A solution we provide to the general public is in the form of a (school-safe) anti-air missile defense system! Totally safe - with the goal of ONLY targetting paper airplanes or other nuissant projectiles. For this system we will be utilizing an FPGA as a controller for the anti-air defense, utilizing a camera to detect the projectile, a laser to point it out, as well as shooting it down with a nerf bullet. For the purchaser/protector, there will also be an option to connect video output to the FPGA board where you can see the locking on of the target with a red box around the plane, outputted to a monitor.
